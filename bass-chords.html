BASS-CHORDS Plugin

Exports *BASS-CHORD-MODEL* to define the following bass guitar chords: 

    [SOLO]   - Single notes
    [OCT]    - Octaves
    [POWER]  - Root & 5th
    [POWER2] - Root, 5th & Octave
    [PER4]   - Perfect 4th
    [MAJ]    - Major
    [7]      - Dominate 7th
    [MIN]    - Minor
